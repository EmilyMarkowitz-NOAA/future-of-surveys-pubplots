2 Analaysis summary

We conducted a literature review using the web of science citation system (search query: https://www.webofscience.com/wos/woscc/summary/7f060455-0bd6-4643-bd2f-2de3ceedb0b2-f52da389/relevance/1). To be included in this analysis, each paper had to contain a long-term fisheries survey or pilot study of a new study Our search returned 1,803 entries, of which contained 2 duplicate results and 361 irrelevant results, leaving 95 relevant articles to categorize. The following categories were used in this analysis:

Gear Type: 3D stereo camera (e.g., stereo-baited remote underwater video (S-BRUV)), Acoustics, Advanced Tech, Beach seine, Beam Trawl, Bongo net, Bottom Trawl, Fisher interviews, Fisheries-Dependant, Gillnets, Grab samples, Hook & Line, Ichthyoplankton, Longline, Multiple, None, Otter Trawl, Pelagic net, Poly Nor’Eastern, four-seam, hard bottom, high-rise bottom trawl (AFSC BTS AI & GOA), Stock assessments, Traps, Uncrewed surface vehicle saildrone, Video
