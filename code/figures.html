We conducted a literature review using the web of science citation system (search query: https://www.webofscience.com/wos/woscc/summary/7f060455-0bd6-4643-bd2f-2de3ceedb0b2-f52da389/relevance/1). To be included in this analysis, each paper had to contain a long-term fisheries survey or pilot study of a new study Our search returned 1,803 entries, of which contained 2 duplicate results and 288 irrelevant results, leaving 95 relevant articles to categorize. The following categories were used in this analysis:
